About Us

Playing for Formosa Music Association was established to create opportunities for young musicians and bring the beauty of classical music to communities across Taiwan. Our musicians, many of whom have studied at prestigious institutions like Juilliard and Harvard, are committed to sharing their passion for music through performances in concert halls, hospitals, schools, and rural communities.
